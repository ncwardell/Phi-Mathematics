- Complex numbers (i emerges from chirality product)
- Matter/antimatter asymmetry (|phi| > |psi|)
- Photon as self-conjugate bridge element
- Fibonacci dynamics --> time
- NegaFibonacci --> psi-strand sign structure
- Hyperbolic spacetime with Minkowski signature
- Hopf fibration with witnessing quantum 1/(4pi)
- Torus identity structure
- Breathing torus: antiphase oscillation of the double helix
- Spin as breath cycle periodicity
- Fermion/boson distinction from strand symmetry
- 4pi spinor double cover from strand exchange
- Hourglass as dynamic vibrational mode
- Dual torus dimensions: spatial (rungs/C) and temporal (depth/d)
- Mass equation decomposition: temporal winding x spatial coupling
- Particle formation as coincidence of spatial resonance + temporal address

- Time (derived: from dynamic self-reference index)

- Matter-antimatter distinction (derived: from double triangle)

- Spin (derived: from breathing oscillation of double helix on torus)
